How AI data centers lead to big electric bills in NY and NJ

New York Post • 3小时前
Households are now competing against power-hungry computer data centers from the likes of Amazon, Google, Meta and Microsoft put strain on the grid to power new artificial intelligence projects. Add in the fact that New York and New Jersey have both shut down nuclear power and pursued expensive green mandates, and customers are seeing a big strain on their wallets.
