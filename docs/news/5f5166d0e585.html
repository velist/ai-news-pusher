Warren Brodey, 101, dies; a visionary at the dawn of the Information Age

The Boston Globe • 4小时前
Warren Brodey, who used his background as a psychiatrist to develop wide-ranging ideas about the liberating possibilities of technology at the dawn of the information age -- ideas that helped lay the groundwork for revolutionary fields like artificial intelligence -- died on Aug. 10 at his home in Oslo, Norway. He was 101.
